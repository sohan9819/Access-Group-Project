Who are we ?

A New Era of Market Friendly Economy has downed in the World. in India too, a wind of policy changes leading to Liberalization, has generated a new Environment for rapid Industrialization. The Engineer reforms have created an atmosphere, congenial to the Industrial Growth in the Country. India with enormous growth in Information Technology & Service Sector has Established itself as a major Service Oriented Country in the World. In recent past Shipping & International Transportation Industry has Grown many folds in India. Currently India is one of the fastest growing Economy in the world & Growing Stronger Everyday.

This widens scope for professionals involved in this Industry to Explore, Develop and Deliver the best possible Services to the world from India.

With view to above - Access Group was incorporated in June, 1997 by Mr. Sanjay Malviya (A Business Management Graduate - having Vast Experience in Shipping Industry - since 1990). The main business objective of the company is to concentrate on International Transportation & Logistics Services.

We Offer : Shipping, Ship Chartering Brokerage, Project & Heavy Lift Transportation, Ship Sale & Purchase Brokerage, Commercial Ship Management, Shipping Agency, Stevedoring, Sea & Air Freight forwarding services for both Imports & Exports, Door to Door Services, NVOCC, LCL Consolidation, Breakbulk, Order Monitoring, Customs Brokerage, Documentation, Packaging, Warehousing, Distribution Management, Transportation & Allied Services.
